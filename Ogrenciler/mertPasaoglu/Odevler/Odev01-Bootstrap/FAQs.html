FAQ

Credit card: When ordering, you select your type of credit card (we accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Diners) and we debit the sum from your credit card account at the time of shipping. Please note that, unfortunately, we cannot accept Maestro, Switch or Laser cards. When paying by credit card, please always use our Secure Server which transfers encoded data. Please check with your Card Company or bank if in doubt. If your order is large and it is your first order by credit card, we may request proof of the card’s details - for your safety as well as ours!

If your order is shipped with DHL You can track your parcel(s) with the following DHL link: dhl.de Enter your 12 digit German (DHL) parcel number starting -166, this can be found in your RealTone customer login centre or your confirmation of shipment. You may find in some European countries that a matchcode will be issued upon arrival in the country of destination. This information is also available in the above DHL link.

This depends very much on the products, but 90% of our orders are processed the same day and shipped within 24 hours. The indication of the product availability on our product pages and in many product lists is an important clue. If items are available and in stock, we can usually send them immediately, all other delivery times are confirmed delivery times from our suppliers, this means if the delivery status shows "2-3 days", the product should arrive in our warehouse within 2-3 days. On rare occasions we are let down by our suppliers and therefore cannot meet the advertised date. We appreciate that this can be frustrating but we are careful about the availability information we publish and endeavour at all times to only show the correct dates. In your customer log in centre, you can always check the status of your orders and if you need something really urgently, please contact us concerning a partial shipment or an alternative product.

We take all the necessary precautions and work with the best transport companies. However, our parcels, and in the worst cases the contents, do occasionally arrive damaged. In the case of a damaged or missing item, we will of course be here to provide assistance. We will ask you for some information, for example: which item is concerned and what state the parcel is in. We will also ask you for some photos of the parcel and of the damaged items, and will ask you to keep the parcel and all the packing material for a possible inspection by the transport agency. With your help and cooperation, we will be able to treat your case quickly and to your satisfaction.

Normally all orders made through our website during the opening hours are processed and confirmed within a few hours. This ensures that orders placed on the website are processed just as quickly as orders placed by phone. Most orders placed before 4 p.m. can be shipped on the same day. Orders placed on our website after the opening hours can sometimes be processed immediately and might even be shipped before we open the next day, but we are unable to guarantee this.
